Certification & Quality Control

ISO 9001:2008

Quality policy is a reflection of the endeavors and aims of the organization.

Quality has always been a benchmark at Marsons. Our uncompromising stance on quality and strict adherence to ISO 9001:2000 standards furnish our customers with the very best and reliable products. Our quality control activities can be summarized as:

Custom order management
Approval system for all the vendors
Strict manufacturing supervision
Testing and inspection

Our testing departments with its hi-end equipment play a decisive role in the implementation of the said program. We have two laboratories, both equipped with modern machineries and precision instruments; tests are conducted seperately wih one laboratory being allotted for high/medium voltage transformers and the other one for medium/low voltage transformers.

Our fully equipped NABL accredited laboratory can efficiently carry out all required routine and type tests (including Impulse lightning tests and Temperature rise tests), for various ratings of Power Transformers up to the range of 160 MVA 220 kV class with various conventional/state of the art/modern equipment.

”Our policy is to manufacture and supply trouble free products in conformance to specification for the satisfaction of our customers. We commit to increase productivity and sales volume and to ensure continual improvement of system, timely delivery of specified quality products and services, at competitive prices, including compliance to product, health, safety and environmental legal & statutory, regulatory and other requirements in a manner that prevents environmental pollution, ill health of the employees – society. We shall set measurable targets for appropriate parameters and shall continually monitor them.”
