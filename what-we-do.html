What We Do

We Manufacture Power and Distribution Transformers ranging from 10 KVA to 160 MVA to 220 KV Class Power Transformers, Furnace Transformer, Dry Type Transformers and various types of special application Transformers.

Our expertise encompasses Design, Development, Manufacture and Testing of Power and Distribution Transformers. Our Transformers of various ratings, which includes 150 MVA 220 kV class have been successfully type tested by reputed laboratories such as CPRI and ERDA.

As a company we recognize that every minute of downtime in power supply due to transformers fault has multiplier effect as not only customers but also electricity suppliers incur financial losses. Our dedicated team of technicians supports the breakdown maintenance in minimum response time. We have ensured that technical and maintenance support is made available to our clients 24/7 across the country through an integrated network of trained engineers and channel partners.

After Sales Services

Transformers manufactured by Marsons come with an initial guarantee period ranging from 2 years to 5 years based on the client’s requirement and the transformer type. In event of any issues, with our highly trained mobile staff, Marsons is able to speedily respond to any service need to honor its guarantee commitment to the equipment across continents.

With availability of Service Transformers and other equipment available to our Project Division, Marsons is able to ensure that the disruption to the customer is minimum. Marsons offers various packages for maintenance of transformers and complete substations. Please contact us for more information on these.
